# Flight-Operations-And-Revenue-Tracker-Tableau

A Tableau dashboard that tracks flight bookings and revenue across routes and time periods. It highlights key KPIs, trends, top routes, and customer booking patterns to help quickly monitor flight performance and revenue health.

Key Features

Total bookings and total revenue tracking

Route wise and airline wise performance

Monthly and daily trend analysis

Customer booking behavior insights

Fully interactive filters for route, airline, date, and category

Tools Used

Tableau Public

Excel/CSV (Data source)

Purpose

To provide a quick, data driven view of flight operations and revenue health for business monitoring and strategic planning.
